The old model has a name. It is called arbitrage.

In the media buying industry, arbitrage means trading against your client's budget: they buy your media low, bill you high, and pocket the spread. It is rarely disclosed and never itemized. Some agencies do it through marked-up CPMs, some through opaque fees, some through both. You cannot audit what you cannot see. Our answer is structural: we take no margin on media at all, so there is nothing to hide and nothing to audit.
